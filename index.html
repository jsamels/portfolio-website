About Me

I am studying Informatics with a cognate in Business and a minor in Human Computer Interaction (HCI) at Indiana University. Throughout my education at Indiana University I have developed skills in coding languages such as Python and HTML, as well as experience in UX/UI design. Through numerous group projects and previous leadership experience, including recruitment chair of my fraternity, I have further developed my skills in communication and creativity. I started my career in landscape design and then eventually moved into UX/UI design, focusing on human-centered designs.
