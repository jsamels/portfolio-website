Effective Altruism IUB

I recently joined Effective Altruism at Indiana University. I will be working with the tech team to create a website for the club, that will allow people to learn about our organization and how they can help. Effective altruism (EA) is a philosophical and social movement that aims to help others by using reason and evidence to identify the best causes to support. EA at IU's mission is to bring Hoosiers together to learn about how we can use our financial and technical skills to maximize our impact on the world, and help those who need it most.
